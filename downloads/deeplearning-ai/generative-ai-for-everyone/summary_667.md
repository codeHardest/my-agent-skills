### 负责任AI的概念与背景

Responsible AI refers to developing and using AI in ways that ethical, trustworthy, and socially responsible. 负责任AI是指以合乎道德、可信赖和社会责任的方式开发和使用人工智能。

Lots of developers, businesses, and governments care about this and have been having conversations and also have been working hard to make sure that AI is built and used responsibly. 众多开发者、企业和政府都关注这一问题，并一直在进行讨论并努力确保AI的开发和使用是负责任的。

Because of all this attention and effort on responsible AI, we've actually made quite a lot of progress on this in the last few years, with for example, many governments and companies publishing frameworks for responsible AI. 由于对负责任AI的关注和努力，在过去几年中我们确实取得了很大进展，例如许多政府和公司发布了负责任AI框架。

But a lot of work still remains. 但仍有许多工作要做。

### 负责任AI的五个关键维度

视频指出，在如何构建负责任AI的细节仍在探索中，但一些共同主题已经浮现。以下是实施负责任AI的关键维度：

**1. 公平性(Fairness)**
First is fairness to ensure that AI doesn't perpetuate or amplify biases. 首先是公平性，确保AI不延续或放大偏见。

**2. 透明度(Transparency)**
Transparency to make sure AI systems and the decisions are understandable to the stakeholders, to the people impacted. 透明度确保AI系统和决策可被利益相关者、受影响的人所理解。

**3. 隐私(Privacy)**
Privacy, protecting user data ensuring confidentiality. 隐私，保护用户数据，确保机密性。

**4. 安全性(Security)**
Security, safeguarding AI systems from malicious attacks. 安全性，保护AI系统免受恶意攻击。

**5. 伦理使用(Ethical Use)**
Lastly, ethical use ensuring the AI is used for beneficial purposes. 最后是伦理使用，确保AI用于有益的目的。

### 实施负责任AI的挑战与建议

One of the challenges of these dimensions, or these principles is that the implementation is not always straightforward. 这些维度或原则的挑战之一是实施并不总是简单直接的。

For example, I think at least a couple thousand years now, humanity has been debating what is ethical and what is not ethical. 例如，我认为至少几千年来，人类一直在争论什么是合乎伦理的，什么不是合乎伦理的。

There is unfortunately no clear mathematical definition of ethical versus unethical behavior. 遗憾的是，对于伦理行为与非伦理行为没有明确的数学定义。

Although, of course, there are many clear cut cases as well. 当然，也有许多明确的案例。

But that's why for individuals, organizations, even countries to adopt responsible AI, there are certain emerging best practices to hope, have the discussion and debate that will lead to better and more responsible decisions, even when sometimes the right thing to do could be ambiguous. 但正因为如此，对于个人、组织甚至国家来说，采用负责任AI有一些新兴的最佳实践，通过讨论和辩论来做出更好、更负责任的决策，即使有时正确答案可能不明确。

### 三个实用建议

**建议一：建立讨论文化**
First, I think it's important to build a culture that encourages discussion and debate on ethical issues. 首先，我认为重要的是建立一种鼓励讨论和辩论伦理问题的文化。

If someone on your team has a concern about the use of responsible AI, it'd be great if they have the freedom to raise that issue to enable the team to maybe make a better decision. 如果团队中的某人对负责任AI的使用有顾虑，他们能够自由提出这个问题，以便团队做出更好的决策。

**建议二：头脑风暴潜在问题**
Second tip is to brainstorm, either by yourself or with your team, or with an even broader group of stakeholders how things could go wrong. 第二个建议是进行头脑风暴，无论是自己还是与团队，或与更广泛的利益相关者一起思考事情可能如何出错。

I've found on many projects that this brainstorming can help identify potential problems and allow the team to mitigate them in advance. 我在许多项目中都发现，这种头脑风暴可以帮助识别潜在问题，并让团队提前减轻风险。

A checklist for brainstorming could be the five dimensions I described on the previous slide. 头脑风暴的清单可以是上一页描述的五个维度。

Could the AI system have issues with fairness, transparency, privacy, security, or ethical use. AI系统是否可能在公平性、透明度、隐私、安全性或伦理使用方面存在问题。

For example, on some of the projects I've worked on, my team has brainstorm in advance if the AI and we deployed could have fairness issues such as if it might exhibit some of the biases that you saw earlier in this course. 例如，在我参与的一些项目中，我的团队会提前进行头脑风暴，看看我们部署的AI是否可能有公平性问题，比如它是否可能表现出你在本课程前面看到的某些偏见。

**建议三：建立多元化团队**
Finally, I encourage you to work of a diverse team and include perspectives from all stakeholders impacted by the AI system. 最后，我鼓励你建立一个多元化团队，并纳入所有受AI系统影响的利益相关者的观点。

For many projects, seeking a diverse set of opinions, as well as speaking with people that could be quite different than myself has allowed my team to understand better the impact of an AI system and led us to make better decisions. 对于许多项目，寻求多元化的意见，以及与与我不同的人交谈，使我的团队能够更好地理解AI系统的影响，并做出更好的决策。

### 实践案例

For example, building systems in healthcare, I found that talking to patients and doctors gave perspectives different than mine and really changed the direction we took our projects in, and working on retail applications, talking to some of the customers as well as the sellers, gave my team new ideas that we wouldn't have had otherwise. 例如，在构建医疗系统时，我发现与患者和医生交谈给了我不同的视角，真正改变了我们项目的方向。在零售应用方面，与客户和销售商交谈，让我的团队获得了我们原本不会有的新想法。

I think this pattern is true for many projects. 我认为这一模式适用于许多项目。

### 行业最佳实践

If you work in a specific industry, such as healthcare, or finance, or media or tech, there may be emerging best practices for responsible AI specific to your industry that could be useful to consult as well as you embark on your project. 如果你在特定行业工作，如医疗、金融、媒体或科技，可能有针对你所在行业的新兴负责任AI最佳实践，在你开展项目时咨询这些最佳实践可能会很有帮助。

### 伦理决策的重要性

I think we all want to use AI to make people better off. 我认为我们都希望使用AI来让人们过得更好。

There's been a few times that I've killed projects that are assessed to be financially sound on ethical grounds. 有几次我终止了一些在财务上被认为是可行的项目，但出于伦理原因。

As you decide what to work on and what not to work on, I hope you keep on considering responsible AI and only work on projects that you think are ethical and that make people better off. 当你决定做什么项目和不做什么项目时，我希望你继续考虑负责任AI，只做你认为合乎伦理且能让人们过得更好的项目。

Now, we're approaching the end of this course. 现在我们接近本课程的尾声了。

Let's go to the next video to see a summary of what we've covered. 让我们进入下一个视频，看看我们所学内容的总结。
